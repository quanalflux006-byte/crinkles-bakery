About Us

Port Harcourt's Trusted Bakery

Crinkles Bakery and Confectionary has been serving the Port Harcourt community with passion and excellence. What started as a small home bakery has grown into New GRA's favorite spot for custom cakes, fresh pastries, and sweet celebrations.
